Hi there! My name is Michael Lippautz and I am currently a software engineer at Google working on the high-performance JavaScript virtual machine V8 as well as the Blink rendering engine.

I am especially interested in:

  • Virtual machines, garbage collection and memory management, compiler construction
  • Concurrent systems, multicore programming
  • Operating systems, virtualization

I received my PhD in December 2015 at the Department of Computer Sciences at the University of Salzburg. My advisor was Christoph Kirsch; my co-advisor was Ana Sokolova. Google generously supported my PhD through the 2014 Google PhD Fellowship in Concurrent Systems.
